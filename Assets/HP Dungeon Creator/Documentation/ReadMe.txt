Thank you for downloading this package.

This pack contains :
 - 17 extra low poly models
 - 18 prefabs
 - 2 1024x1024 textures (PNG)
 - 2 materials
 - 1 example scene
 
 This asset is really easy to use, you just have to drag the tiles into your scene and fit them together to build custom dungeons.
 You can play with the example scene to explore the possibilities of this pack and discover the differents ways to match the prefabs to create your own dungeon.
